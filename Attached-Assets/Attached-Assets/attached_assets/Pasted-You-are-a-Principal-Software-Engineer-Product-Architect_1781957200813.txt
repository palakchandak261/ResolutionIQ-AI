REAL-WORLD PROBLEM

Across cities and municipalities, citizens face major difficulties while reporting civic problems.

Examples:

• Potholes remain unrepaired for months
• Garbage dumping complaints are ignored
• Water leakages waste thousands of liters daily
• Broken streetlights create safety risks
• Illegal constructions continue unchecked
• Sewage overflow creates health hazards

The core issue is not that governments don't receive complaints.

The real issue is:

1. Citizens don't know which department is responsible.
2. Complaints are written poorly and lack actionable details.
3. Multiple duplicate complaints flood the system.
4. Governments cannot prioritize issues effectively.
5. Language barriers prevent proper communication.
6. No predictive intelligence exists to identify infrastructure failures before they occur.

As a result:

• Public trust decreases.
• Municipal resources are wasted.
• Infrastructure deteriorates faster.
• Resolution times increase.
• Citizens stop reporting problems.

================================================

PROPOSED SOLUTION

ResolutionIQ AI is an AI-powered Civic Intelligence Platform.

It acts as an intelligent layer between citizens and governments.

Instead of simply collecting complaints, it:

• Understands complaints
• Routes complaints
• Prioritizes complaints
• Predicts infrastructure risks
• Helps governments take action faster
